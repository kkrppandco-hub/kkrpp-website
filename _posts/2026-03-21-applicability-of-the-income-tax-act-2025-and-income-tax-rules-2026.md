Effective Date and Applicability

<!--EndFragment-->



<!--StartFragment-->

The **Income Tax Act, 2025** comes into force from **1 April 2026**, and applies from the **tax year 2026–27 onwards**.

* It **replaces the Income-tax Act, 1961 entirely**.
* All income earned **on or after 1 April 2026** will be governed by the new Act.
* Income earned **before this date** continues to be governed by the old law.

The **Income Tax Rules, 2026** are also effective from **1 April 2026**, providing procedural and compliance guidance for implementing the Act.
